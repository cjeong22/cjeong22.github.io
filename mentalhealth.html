Mental Health and Recruiting

Introduction

These are my views on how to approach mental health during software engineering recruiting. All thoughts are my own; feel free to disagree.

Could I do Better?

If you're like me this is a question that always lingers in the front of your mind. After going without an internship, I was ecstatic when I landed JPMorgan Chase & Co, until I thought "could I do better?" After wrapping up my internship and landing an offer from Capital One, I was delighted, until I thought "could I do better?" I'm not going to lie and say no. Yes, you can always do better. However, there are also diminishing returns that come from doing better. Of course, getting an internship is much better than not getting an internship; internships are experience, not having an internship is not getting experience. Questionable startup to big bank? Better, but not as much of an improvement as getting an internship versus no internship. Big bank to mid-sized tech company? Better, but not as much of an improvement as big bank versus questionable startup. Keep
